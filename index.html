Great Pyramids Circuit

Each circuit contains 7 sets of 3 exercises with a cardio "rest".
Start with a lower weight, increase until you can only do 2-4 reps, then decrease back down again.

After your first set of exercise one, do ab exercises with high repetitions.
Then do exercise two.
Followed by 60 seconds of cardio.
Repeat until you are back to your starting weight.
Do 3 full circuits with 9 different exercises to complete the Great Pyramids Circuit.
